Some most frequently asked questions.

What is Triads App?

Triads app is a social media platform designed specifically for gamers. It allows you to connect with other players, share your gaming experiences, discover new games, join communities, and stay updated on the latest gaming news.

How do I create an account?

Creating an account is easy! Simply download the app from the App Store or Google Play, open it, and follow the on-screen instructions to sign up using your email.

Is Triads free to use?

Yes! Triads is free to download and use. However, we offer in-app purchases for exclusive features like Triads store.

How do I share my gaming clips and screenshots?

Sharing your gaming moments is easy! Tap the “+” button at the top-most part of the home screen, select the video or screenshot you want to share, add some title, and post it for your followers to see.
